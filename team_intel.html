Categories where a team is within 2.0 points of the next team up, making the next standings point realistically attainable. Higher totals usually mean a team is more live and competitive in the current race.

Actual roto points minus expectation from tight category clusters. Positive means results are running ahead of that baseline.

Movement from rank changes and category swings during the season. A lower value means more stable performance.
