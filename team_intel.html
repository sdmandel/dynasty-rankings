Tier
Active chase band means close enough to first place to move into the title race: Contender, Fringe, Stagnating, or Rebuilding.
Reachable Points
Categories where a team is within 2.0 points of the next team up, making the next standings point realistically attainable. Higher totals usually mean a team is more live and competitive in the current race.
Luck Delta
Actual roto points minus expectation from tight category clusters. Positive means results are running ahead of that baseline. Negative means if you set your lineup you'd be doing better.
Volatility
Movement from rank changes and category swings during the season. A lower value means more stable performance.
